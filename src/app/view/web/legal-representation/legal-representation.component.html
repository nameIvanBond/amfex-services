Legal Representation

Emirates Wealth is licensed and regulated by the Securities & Commodities Authority (SCA), to act as a legal representative of foreign investment funds. As your legal representative we will liaise with the SCA on your behalf and constantly update your files such as the offering documents, prospectus, and investment policies as well as manage your annual renewals. Further, we represent your funds towards any kind investors.

We offer professional representation services for foreign fund houses and asset management companies (ACM) who wish to promote and distribute their funds in or from the UAE to the GCC and the wider MEANSA region.

We support you to achieve the regulatory approval required for the distribution of funds to retail or qualified investors in the region and further to expand your distribution network by providing knowledge about the regional fund industry. We help you to achieve a successful market entry and expansion of your fund distribution locally and globally.

Regulatory Notifications (SCA)

As your legal representative, we are obliged to notify SCA about;
  • Registration of new funds or sub-funds
  • Change of legal form of investment fund
  • Merger and liquidation of investment fund
  • Deregistration of investment funds and/or sub-funds
  • Notification of new or cancellation of share classes
  • Delay in redemption of units/shares
  • Change of legal representative or paying agent
  • Any measures implemented by a foreign regulatory authority against the investment fund
  • Valuation errors and their rectification
